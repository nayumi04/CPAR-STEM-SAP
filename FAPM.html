The Progress of Medicine in the Philippines by Carlos “Botong” Francisco

Carlos "Botong" Francisco's The Progress of Medicine in the Philippines is a mural showing the evolution of medicine and healthcare, from tribal healing to modern medicine used today. the painting features indigenous groups, spanish priests or friars and doctors of today, it was commissioned by the philippine general hospital in 1953, as it commemorates how filipino medical and healthcare progressed overtime. The painting was reproduced in 2007 to preserve the painting.
